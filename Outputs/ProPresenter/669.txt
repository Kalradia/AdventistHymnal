The Lord Bless You and Keep You

Verse 1
The Lord bless you and keep you,
the Lord life His countenance upon you,

and give you peace;
the Lord make His face to shine upon you,

and be gracious unto you,
be gracious,

the Lord be gracious unto you.
Amen.
